Kakashi Hatake

Kakashi Hatake is one of the higher level Ninjas in the hiddenleaf village. He is the teacher (sensei) of Uzumaki Naruto, Haruno Sakura and Uchiha Sasuke. His teacher was the fourth hokage, Minato Namikaze and his teammates were Obito Uchiha and Rin Nohara. His rival is Might Guy. His father is the white fang of the leaf. His one and only original technique is Raikiri (lightning blade).
